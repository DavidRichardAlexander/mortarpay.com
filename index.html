How it works

Four steps. Complete clarity.

A structured process that protects both homeowners and contractors from start to finish.

01

Create a contract

Define milestones, deliverables, and timeline. Both homeowner and contractor agree on scope and expectations.

02

Homeowner funds project

Homeowner makes a single secure payment. Funds are held by Mortar Pay until milestones are approved.

03

Verify work

Contractor submits progress. Homeowner reviews and approves, or flags for discussion.

04

Funds released

Approved milestone payments go directly to contractor's bank account quickly. Repeat for each milestone.
